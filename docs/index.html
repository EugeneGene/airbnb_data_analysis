Key Findings

After analyzing the data, we discovered some noteworthy findings:

  • Properties with higher ratings tend to have more bookings.
  • Houses located near popular tourist attractions have higher rental rates.
  • Superhosts tend to receive more positive reviews and have higher occupancy rates.
  • The presence of specific amenities (e.g., pool, Wi-Fi) impacts the rental price.

Conclusion

This data analysis provides valuable insights into the performance of Airbnb houses. Understanding the factors that influence house performance can help hosts optimize their listings and enhance the overall guest experience.
